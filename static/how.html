Loops automatically. Your connection blips on for a few seconds, the backlog fans out to Cerebras in parallel across the key pool, every answer streams back into its waiting bubble, then it closes.

A Jetson Orin Nano is the hub — it runs Ferry, the on-device model, and the chat UI. Weak client devices join its WiFi hotspot (always on, no internet needed), so the chat never goes dark. Only the hub's uplink flickers — and when it opens, the backlog bursts to Cerebras and streams back to the client.
